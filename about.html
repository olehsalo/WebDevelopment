Why am I here?

The idea of this webpage is to help you realise that you do not need fancy ingredients to cook something great.Everything you need is already laying around in your fridge and in cupboards, you just need to combine it the right way. And this is why you are here. Just type the ingredients you have and we will tell you how to create a masterpiece out of them from the comfort of your home. So what are you waiting for: it is time to eat!
